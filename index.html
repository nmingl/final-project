ABOUT KAPE BANQUETA

Kape Banqueta began with the inspiring journey of Mr. Kiko Matthew. Once a passionate videographer and photographer, Kiko captured life’s most meaningful moments through his lens. However, when the pandemic struck and events were suddenly cancelled, he found himself at a crossroads.

During this time, he discovered a new passion for coffee. Starting as a barista at Starbucks, Kiko honed his craft, learning the art and science behind every perfect cup. After resigning, he decided to bring that experience home—literally.

What began as a simple pop-up garage café on their home’s sidewalk was originally meant to be called La Banqueta (Spanish for “the sidewalk”). But as it grew, the name naturally evolved into something that felt closer to home: Kape Banqueta.

From humble beginnings serving coffee on the street, Kiko’s dedication and creativity helped Kape Banqueta blossom into a cozy, fully established café—where every cup is brewed with passion, community, and a story that started right by the sidewalk.
